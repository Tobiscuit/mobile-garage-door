# Visual Asset Prompts

**For Model:** Nano Banana Pro (or Flux/Midjourney)
**Style Goal:** Educational, High-Tech, Professional, Minimalist.
**Use Case:** Slide decks or client branding to explain complex tech simply.

---

## 1. Concept: "The Digital Handshake" (AI Handoff)
*Explaining how the AI seamlessly passes data to the booking system.*

> **Prompt:** A minimalist isometric 3D render. On the left, a floating smartphone screen displaying a garage door diagnostic scan with a soft blue glow. On the right, a glass tablet showing a "Service Ticket Approved" checkmark in warm gold. A single, elegant beam of light connects the two devices, representing instant data transfer. Clean white background, soft studio lighting, high-end corporate tech aesthetic. Keep it simple and uncluttered.

---

## 2. Concept: "The Edge Network" (Speed)
*Explaining why the site is so fast (global distribution).*

> **Prompt:** A stylized, minimalist globe made of dark matte metal. The surface is dotted with hundreds of tiny, glowing cyan lights (representing edge servers). A unified mesh of light connects all the dots instantly. No heavy text, just the glowing network against a deep charcoal background. Cyberpunk-lite, clean lines, data visualization style.

---

## 3. Concept: "The Semantic Garden" (SEO Growth)
*Explaining how the AI grows content without repeating itself.*

> **Prompt:** A metaphorical "digital garden" growing out of a sleek laptop. The plants are made of translucent glass and fiber optics. Each leaf represents a unique piece of content. A small, abstract beam of light (the AI) is tending to the garden, pruning duplicate branches. The scene is bright, airy, and optimistic. Colors: Emerald green, tech blue, and white. High fidelity, 8k render.

---

## 4. Concept: "Context Compression" (The Semantic Header)
*Explaining how the AI reads millions of articles instantly by only looking at the "DNA" header.*

> **Prompt:** A macro close-up of a stream of data. In the center, a single, glowing, diamond-like crystal labeled "SEMANTIC HEADER". Behind it, a blurry, dense cloud of text (the full article) is being compressed into this single point of light. The crystal is sharp, refracted, and intensely bright. The background is a deep void. Represents extreme efficiency and density. 8k, hyper-realistic, sci-fi data visualization.

---

## 5. Concept: "The Unfair Advantage" (Proximity)
*Archetype: The Magician / The Ruler*
*Explaining that we win because we are closer.*

> **Prompt:** A high-speed race track. On the far left, a weary runner (Legacy Server) is sprinting from a distant mountain labeled "Virginia". On the right, at the finish line in a modern city (Houston), a Magician simply *appears* in a flash of blue light, holding the trophy (the website). He didn't run; he teleported. The crowd goes wild. Represents: The overwhelming power of physical proximity vs raw speed.

---

## 6. Concept: "The Command Center" (Dashboard)
*Explaining the unified view.*

> **Prompt:** A wide-angle view of a futuristic glass desk. On it sits a single monitor displaying a clean, organized dashboard interface functionality. Holographic charts float slightly above the screen showing "Revenue", "Bookings", and "Efficiency". The background is a blurred, high-end office. The focus is on clarity and control. Professional photography style, depth of field.
